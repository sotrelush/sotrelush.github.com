Little Mommy Interactive Doll

Nov 12, 2011 @ 3:17 p.m.

When you have a little princess in your life chances are high she really loves babies. If you have ever watched a child when she catches sight of a baby, you know this is true. This is just one reason we parents get baby dolls as toys for our little girls and also our little boys these days. They are an ideal toy which helps our kids learn nurturing skills. Your little girl might have some baby dolls of her own with which she routinely plays. But there is one type that's sure to be a favorite of hers. The Little Mommy doll is so life-like and nice it's sure to be a hit.

Dolls used to be plain and typical with no real distinguishing changes between one type and another (till Cabbage Patch dolls that is). But times have definitely changed. Mattel has come out with this Little Mommy doll series that offers a choice to anyone - no matter what their particular taste or personality might be. There actually is something for everyone so making your selection simply comes down to what your little girl likes or which one you think will help her learn something. If she enjoys a doll that sits and lets her do all the entertaining then there are a lot to select from. They come in a large number of colors and fashions with pink clothing being the main color for the doll clothing. Just a few do have clothes with a unique combination of colors.

If, on the other hand, your little kid enjoys an interactive doll that can function somewhat on her own, then there are some of those sort of Little Mommy dolls available, too. Some kinds may sing, while others play fun games like peek-a-boo. And in case she needs a playmate that moves around Mattel has you covered there as well. There are even some dolls that walk their very own dog. All of these movements are made possible because of built-in motion sensors. At night time, when it's time to go to sleep just turn off the sensor and your little girl's interactive doll could lie next to her while she dreams. Not only do some of the Little Mommy dolls move in different ways and speak different words, there are some which can eat "real" food and juice. Obviously, what goes in must come out. This means you would be wanting some additional diapers for your little girl to change.
